About Us - RefugeeReady

About Ready Refugee

RefugeeReady is dedicated to helping displaced persons rebuild their lives. We provide essential support like housing, healthcare, education, and employment opportunities, while connecting refugees with local communities for a smoother integration process.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower refugees by offering resources, support, and a sense of community that will help them rebuild their lives with dignity and independence.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every displaced person has access to safe housing, meaningful work, healthcare, and the support of a community that welcomes them with open arms.

Our Goals

    -Provide housing support for 10,000 refugees in the next 5 years.

    -Connect 5,000 refugees with job opportunities within their first year of resettlement.

    -Offer mental health services to at least 80% of refugees served.
